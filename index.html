Our Story

Central Grill & BBQ was founded in 2005 by Devansh Pratap Singh. We currently own and operate 138 outlets in India, 5 outlets in UAE, 1 outlet in Malaysia, 1 outlet in Singapore and 1 outlet in Thailand. One of the leading casual dining chains in India, Central Grill & BBQ pioneered the concept of over the table barbeque live grills embedded in dining tables – allowing guests to grill their own barbecue’s right at their tables.

What we do

Indian cuisine is a natural part of the diet in the markets in which the Central Grill & BBQ Restaurants operate. We continue to offer new menu options, both vegetarian and non-vegetarian, largely based on Indian cuisine and use seasonal customer preferences to introduce new dishes. We also run popular food festivals at our Central Grill & BBQ Restaurants, thereby offering our customers a range of Indian and international cuisines. Our fixed price all you can eat concept at our Central Grill & BBQ Restaurants offers our customers a varying spread of consistently delicious food at a value-oriented price in a pleasant and casual dining environment.
